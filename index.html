Detailed Information



Appearance
Kina's body doesn't have the distinct hourgglass figure that a lot of girls have, her shape making more of a curve than a point to her waist and leads down into another smooth curve to her hips and long legs. Her skin is a light peach shade, some considering her to be pale. Her platinum blonde hair frames her skin causing her to look a little darker than she really looks.
Her facial structure is distinctly European, her narrow nose pricking up at the tip as well as her plump pink lips. Kina's cheekbones are the widest part of her face which causes it to have a slight heart shape that leads into her narrow jaw which fits her overall frame perfectly. Her baby blue eyes are often described as piercing.

Personality
Kina is a fairly kind individual, often putting the needs of others beyond her own. She would do next to anything to help another if the need proposed itself to her, given it be something she is able to help with. Calm and contemplative, she lost most her impulsiveness and temperament issues quickly after arriving at Ulreich.
Kina still has her sense of humor and is still slightly feisty but she gained a bit more gentleness. She now strives to uphold peace and promote prosperity but is definitely not above fighting for what she believes is right, especially it is keeps those she loves out of danger.

History

Before Ulreich University

It was a version of the new world unlike any other. It had been perfectly aligned with the original New World’s timeline before straying off into a unique path. For this version, there was no Renaissance era. So, instead of an explosion of art, the world had a technological boom. This allowed the human race to have access to higher forms of technology, and fast forwarded into the present time, the scene was unbelievable.

It was exactly what many fantasy/science fiction storybooks had foretold for hundreds or thousands of years into the future, although it was simply April third in the year 2057. In London, England, a small baby girl was born with bright blonde hair and striking blue eyes. Kina, as the child would be named, was an odd yet caring creature. She would often bring in stray animals that she found on her own little adventures around the street block that she lived on to her father who would patch them up and nurse them back to health until they found an owner worthy of the girl’s standards.

The only reason her father was around the house full time was because her mother, Alice Buvelle, was a high ranking Officer in the British Tech Navy. The Tech Navy was a branch of the military that oversaw Sea warfare, as well as pushing the exploration of the sea floor through the help of specialized undersea machines crafted out of a high durability metal alloy that allowed them to delve as far deep into the ocean without succumbing to underwater pressure and causing an internal collapse of the vehicle. Over time the relationship between mother and daughter dwindled into nothing.

Kina had many friends growing up, and even attended a highly expensive private school that offered the best education in all of England. These friends of hers would tell her stories about the holidays that they spent together with their happy family and she realized that..comparing her personal experiences to her friends’, she didn’t have a very happy family. Her mother wasn’t home very often, and when she was the woman barraged her father with verbal abuse. They never celebrated the holidays, never visited family for functions. She lived a closed and sad life.

Around the age of fifteen, horrifying news struck her like a cold slap to the face. Her mother had sold falsified evidence that the United Kingdom had been testing out nukes that were laced with explosive mana, in effect creating a radioactive bomb, on sparse locations in Russia and the United States, and caused many unborn, newborns, and young children to develop grotesque physical mutations. She did this for magic power, which both angered and terrified the girl. In a flash, a war was upon them.

The war itself wasn’t fought with technology, for the most part the most dominating factor was magic, but technology certainly supported it. The battles fought throughout the United Kingdom were so high in mana concentration that it caused a temporal rift to open up outside of Dover. Eager to escape the havoc, Kina raced through it without a second thought. This rift transported her to the far reaches of the Free Lands in the Esmerelda Isles. This was unknown to her at the time, but quickly she found Ulreich University and vowed that as soon as she was strong enough to return, she would.

She would return to end her mother’s madness, to restore peace to her now broken world.

And return she did. Two and a half years after her departure, with many of her newfound companions, Kina made the trek back up to the Free Lands' mountain ranges. They passed through the rift back into the land of her birth and after completeing the trial of defeating her mother's power conduits, they were able to breach the shield surrounding London. There they would find Kina's mother, a now extremely powerful mage wielding two mixed trees of maximum rank. Through much effort, they were able to defeat her without sustaining any losses. They returned back to Ulreich where Kina would fall into a depressive spiral.

For months she hid away in the safety and isolation of her dorm room, barely eating or interacting with the outside world. She went through a series of suicidal thoughts, but it hit her. Why kill herself? After all she'd been through, why put the people she loved in pain because of her own selfish desire to escape the guilt of killing her mother. She had done it for a good cause, it had taken its toll on her, but now she had a decision to make. End it all, or get back up and work through the pain, the suffering, to restore herself back to her former glory as a gifted Faith mage protecting and healing those in need. She chose the latter. Now she returns to her mission to achieving maximum rank to be as much use to the school as possible.

Mana Manipulation
The most basic class of magic, involving the direct manipulation of Mana, is available to all mages. Raw Mana is a mundane source of magic with no special qualities, but offers some level of versatility. The amount of Mana a mage is capable of replenishing or expelling is dependent on their overall level.

Unique Archmage Mana Type
Earth Warden Mana is a combination of both her Faith Mana and her Geomancy Mana types. This mana is capable of being manifested in both physical and essence-based forms while also being capable of being infused into both sentient and non-sentient beings. Direct contact with Earth Warden mana in its base state without spells will automatically begin healing minor injuries such as scrapes, small cuts, bruises, etc., and when in its base form it may have a physical durability of up to iron strength. Can not be used to harm in any way, shape, or form.

Faith Mana
Mana which cannot do damage in any form. This mana is usually very warm and pleasant, and sometimes even gives off relaxing scents.

Earth Mana
Mana which manifests itself as dirt, mud, dust, etc.

Personalized Mana
Kina's mana, when visible to the naked eye, appears as an aura of bright gold, yet has a thin inner and outer border of a muted shade of brown that blends perfectly into the gold hues. Browns and other nude shades can be seen as small particles drifting inside of her aura.

Mana Weave
The simple act of moving one's mana pool through their own body. Channeling one's mana

Mana Spark
A basic mana projectile that can be used offensively or defensively. It causes no real damage other than a small sting when hit directly in the flesh.

Mana Pane
When focusing one's mana before them and swirling it in a vortex quickly and violently, one may allow their mana to harden, causing a thin pane to form. This spell can only be used defensively and the power it can block is minimal, ranking at iron toughness. This spell can be learned by anyone of mage rank and above, but upon reaching high mage, one may cast two of these at a time, one for each hand.

Mana Beacon
This spell is cast by concentrating on one's mind and projecting mana outwards like a sudden flash of light. Although this does not produce any visual effects, it sends a beacon-like pulse of mana outwards from ones body which can be picked up an interpreted by other mages who have learned this spell. It does not convey any message, rather a simple alarm. This can be used to send a cry for help to people within a set area of your character.

Spirit Beacon
An upgrade to Mana Beacon, rather than projecting one's mana from the mind in a pulse, this requires focus to be gathered to one's very core of being until released into the world around them. It scatters outward in a pulse like Mana Beacon, but is targeted specifically at Faith Mages, Necromancers and Spiritualists, intended to only be sensed by mages who follow those trees. While it opens up the possibility of being detected over a vaster distance (still within the same chat but a considerably larger range, with some very specific exceptions), it also offers the opportunity to warn aforementioned mages that a spirit may be disconnected from the body (recently or will be soon) and will require a guide back for revival. Upon reaching Expert Mage, focusing on this spell as it is being cast will allow the caster to choose which specific type of previously listed mage (Faith, Necromancer, Spiritualist) they desire to be able to sense their beacon.

Gel Sphere
By creating a mana spark and continuing to weave your mana around the spark like a web, eventually the orb one forms will expand into a small sphere of an incredibly adhesive substance. Once casted, this spell lasts for three posts, and has a toughness of aluminum.

Summon Medium
A spell that allows a user to transport their medium from a previous location into their hands. Only one medium can be used at a time with this spell, and can include HMM's, as well as basic objects and weapons. To change one's medium, one must use it at least once before being able to actively summon it.

Mana Meditation
This spell takes two posts, the first post charges the spell, the second post activates it. If a mage meditates on whatever is causing them pain or discomfort--meaning they focus their undivided attention inward or to whatever specific part of their body is hurting--and slowly weave their mana to that particular place, they will be able to activate this spell. Mana Meditation increases a mage's natural pain tolerance slightly and can calm whatever natural shock they may be experiencing, allowing them to function better. Natural shock meaning the universal spell won't work on magic based shock

Mana Rehabilitation
This spell takes two posts, the first post charges the spell, the second post activates it. If a mage temporarily focuses on whatever is causing them pain or discomfort--meaning they focus their most of their attention inward or to whatever specific part of their body is hurting or being affected by an external source--and weave their mana to that particular place, they will be able to activate this spell. Mana Rehabilitation increases a mage's natural pain tolerance by x1.5 and can calm whatever natural shock or magic based shock (spells that cause pain/shock/discomfort of up to Mage rank) that part of their body may be experiencing. (Can take a tiny bit of edge off higher ranked spells, but not very much, will mainly be ineffective to anything higher than mage rank.)

Mana Parry
In a complicated set of movements, the caster must create a solidified pane of mana upon an appendage or object, and swipe it against an incoming projectile. Upon contact, the caster has a split second to match their mana levels with the opposing spells, which causes the projectile to meld with the casters mana and remove all secondary effects from the energy. This causes the spell to fly off in a random direction, reverted to base mana but still powerful enough to do damage based on the rank of the attack. Casters who use this can only do so on spells of their rank and below, and to do so costs the exact same amount of mana as the spell in question, so one should keep this in mind before parrying anything. Projectiles that have special effects such as detonation, or abilities that can be triggered mid flight can be activated before a spell is able to be parried.

Mana Retaliate
An upgrade to Mana Parry, this technique requires the user to weave their magic into a point of contact (such as their hand or a medium) and double the amount of mana within an incoming projectile. Upon swiping the point of contact against the opposing force, the extra mana will override the attack and transform it into an orb-like blob of magic. The caster of Retaliate must push this slow-moving orb back toward them, and break it open before it makes contact with their body. This can be done by any basic projectile, such as mana parry, and will shatter the magic into three separate and more solid components. These shattered pieces are about the size of a fist, and shaped in an almost arrow-like manner; they fly at a relatively high speed in intricate and hard to follow patterns that eventually track the original attackers location for up to 60 feet away. Upon reaching five feet from their target, the three shards scatter, sending the brightest of the trio at the front of the attacker, while the other two dull projectiles attempt to pincer each of their sides. The frontal projectiles strength remains the same power as the original retaliated attack, and the two side projectiles work at about a rank lower. This cannot be used against projectiles above the casters rank.

Mana Manipulation and Universal Spells

Faith


Faith mages are a completely support-oriented group of mages. Those who practice Faith hold the ability to mend wounds, lift curses and illnesses, bestow temporary magical boons to their allies, or create Holy Equipment which provide a permanent boon. Faith mages are also highly skilled in defensive arts, and are capable of erecting magical barriers. However, faith mana is completely non hostile, and no faith spell can ever cause harm to another being (unless faith magic specifically harms them).

Heal
By projecting her mana through the palms of her hands, she floods the recipients body with white mana. The mana mends broken bones, internal bleeding, flesh wounds, and the like. The larger and more devastating the wound, the more mana the spell takes. This can not regenerate lost limbs.

Cleanse
Another spell transmitted via touch. Cleanse targets both magic and physical ailments such as induced blindness, poisons, curses, and other effects. This spell can only cure spells equal to or less than Kina's rank. Does not affect spells she has no knowledge of.

Fortitude
Blesses an ally target, increasing their physical abilities by 1.5x -- capping at 3x ( strength, endurance, vitality, speed ) and also doubles their pain tolerance. Lasts for five posts and requires some form of contact to cast on someone as well as requiring the target to be within 25 feet of her to retain their buff.

Angelic Bulwark
By extending her mana from her body in a bubble shape, she forms a thick, protective bubble of mana, it's boundaries distinguished by it's dull golden glow. The spell is like a one-way mirror when it comes to casting spells; if a spell is cast from outside, the bubble absorbs it depending on it's magnitude and rank, and spells cast from inside pass freely. The shield may be passed like air, meaning that inside, it's open season. Can absorb up to one High Mage ranked spell ( varies depending on spell strength. )

Sanctuary
Sending a burst of Faith mana into the ground, Kina blesses it in a thirty foot radius around her body, causing the ground to crack in a spiderweb pattern with light bursting from the cracks. While coating the bodies of selected targets whom are bathed in said light, it surrounds their body in a skin-tight suit of highly flexible and durable faith mana which acts as a defensive barrier. A maximum of five targets may be affected by this barrier at one time so long as they stay within the radius of the spell, and it gets progressively stronger depending on how many people it is being cast on while varying in overall mana cost for the same reason. I.E. 2 people get titanium-grade barriers, three people will get cobalt barriers, and five people will all get steel barriers. Each barrier may absorb up to one attack of its respective durability before breaking, and while affected by a barrier the target will receive a visionary buff that allows them to see through lesser ranking darkness also based on the strength of their shield ( lower grade barriers can see through darkness of their own durability rank or lower for up to an extra ten feet in front of them in complete darkness regardless of their barrier strength. ) Like the visionary buff, the spell itself emits a light which can pierce through darkness of equal rank or lower, unable to do so through stronger darkness, and will illuminate the area a good few feet around each crack just enough to be allow for basic -- albeit limited -- vision.

Infusion
At the expense of the caster’s own mana by charging for one post and releasing it in a burst in a twenty foot radius around her, those hit will acquire a buff that allows them to use mana more efficiently, to use more spells without wasting as much mana. Infusion drops the average casting cost of the user’s next spells cast by one rank if equal to or below High Mage, or even two ranks if the user is equal to or below Mage rank. Infusion may affect up to four people at one time, who all have to stay within a thirty foot radius of the caster to keep their buff. This buff lasts for four posts, and rather than just buffing their next spell, it reduces the cost of all future spells cast while this buff is active on them, and requires one post to cool down.

Apotheosis
A powerful buff that may be applied to barriers, rather than a person, this spell causes a strong defensive barrier over a pre-existing one through required contact with it via one of her own spells or direct contact with her body. This barrier allows the previous one to absorb up to one extra attack of High Mage ranking before breaking, or up to two if the barrier is of Mage rank. If the caster leaves a twenty foot radius of the buffed barrier then the buff will deactivate prematurely. Apotheosis lasts for four posts and then goes on a two post cool down timer.

Sensory Pulse
By releasing a pulse of faith mana into the target, the spell works like echolocation. The mana bounces off of every part of them and returns to Kina, allowing her to "see" any internal and external injuries such as internal bleeding, respiratory problems, etc. This spells works to find both physical and magic induced problems and is able to detect harmful spells of up to HM+ rank. Does not detect spells that are intentionally hidden by the caster.
