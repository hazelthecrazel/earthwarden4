Detailed Information



Appearance
This Faith mage lacks the desired hourglass shape that some women have, her narrow shoulders tapering down into her thin waist that accentuates her hips and gives her more of a pear shape than anything else. She is blessed with long legs bound in fair peachy flesh and she walks with the grace of a dancer, platinum blonde locks falling around her body that makes her appear a tad bit darker than she really is. Kina has distinct facial features somewhat European in origin with perfect symmetry. Her cheekbones are high and proud, her face ovular with a defined jawline and a rounded chin. Kina’s lips are plump and pink, complimenting the shades of pink that decorate her cheeks and nose. Her eyes are almond shaped and are a startling shade of sky blue that are often described as piercing but with an odd sense of warmth about them.

Personality
Kina has come a very long way from the start of her Ulreich journey, having gone through hard losses and proud gains over the past several years that has turned her into the woman she is now. She viewed Ulreich as a means of escape and had about her a strong sense of ambition. She wanted to become the strongest mage anyone had ever seen while still being only sixteen at the time. She was incredibly temperamental and antagonistic to her peers until people walked into her life that changed her permanently.
After the loss of her love interest, a good friend, and several respected peers, Kina’s views on life and priorities changed. She now strives to uphold peace amongst her fellow students and will do whatever she can to help someone in need regardless of whether or not she gains anything from it. However she is not above fighting for what she believes is right, especially if it keeps those she loves out of harm’s way.

History

Before Ulreich University

It was a version of the New World just like any other, perfectly aligned and on the same path until there was a slight… deviation. A small plague made its way around several parts of Europe, most commonly affecting babies and children and subsequently killing off all of the great artists from the time just before the Renaissance era began. Without these artists there was no Renaissance which meant no great artistic revolution. Instead there was a technological boom, kickstarting a world no one from the time could have imagined.
The Inventor’s Epoch gave birth to thousands of new items and the rediscovery of magic, snowballing until present-day where it was a scene just like human Science Fiction/Fantasy books portrayed the distant future to be. It was April 3rd in London, England, where a small baby girl born with a head of bright blonde hair and beautiful blue eyes was birthed. Kina, as she would be named, was a peculiar child with a heart as big as the sun was bright. She often brought home stray animals that she found on her many excursions away from her home to nurse back to health with her Father until they found an owner worthy of Kina’s standards

Her Father was around the house full-time which was a blessing since her Mother was never around, always off and sometimes out of the country for work. Alice Buvelle, her Mother, worked as a high ranking officer in the British Tech Navy. This was a branch of the British military that oversaw Sea Warfare as well as the push for the exploration of the sea floor through the help of underwater machines crafted out of a magic-enhanced metal alloy that was highly resistant to underwater pressure. Since Alice was hardly ever around the relationship between Mother and Daughter dwindled into nothing.

The one thing that her parents agreed on was that they send Kina to the most expensive private school in England. She was taught well and had many friends, but whenever holiday breaks came around and they returned back to school she’d always hear them talking about what wondrous times they had. Kina had no such experiences. Instead, whenever Alice returned home there was always arguing, even on the holidays, where her Mother barraged her Father with verbal abuse until he couldn’t take it anymore and left them both. She withdrew from her studies since there was no around to be supportive of her, causing her to lose the friends she’d made as she lived on her own in that sad and closed house. Several years passed, Alice not even bothering to return home for the holidays at that point, where the only thing Kina really had to talk to were the paintings of happy memories from before her Mother became.. Vile.

Days after Kina turned 15 horrifying news struck her like a hot iron; her mother had sold incriminating evidence that the United Kingdom had been testing out nuclear bombs laced with explosive mana that created a devastatingly powerful weapon. Radiation seeped from it where they struck in sparse areas in the United States and Russia, causing newborns and young children to develop grotesque mutations. It was revealed to her that it was done under the promise of money and power from a corrupt member of the United States government. They wanted their resources, their military, and by doing so they’d have to break the U.K. with war which was upon them in a flash.
The war was fought predominantly with technology infused with varying types of incredibly powerful magic which saturated the battlefields. Such intense concentrations of mana was incredibly dangerous for the world itself and so life found a way to adapt. In order to keep corruption, a deadly illness affecting both sentient and nonsentient things alike, at bay it created a link between itself and another world to funnel the infestive energies at a balanced rate into it. At the aftermath of a major battle outside the small town of Dover, one of these gateways named Temporal Rifts appeared in the heart of the neighboring forest. Kina dangerously watched the battle from the safety of the forest and when she discovered this ripple in the air she took her chances and walked through it to escape her perils, leaving her world behind trapped in time at the exact moment she left.

She appeared on a stormy night at the top of a mountain in the ranges of the Free Lands in the Esmerelda Isles, a world unknown to her, and as she descended into the thickets she encountered a hunting party of the vicious natives who didn’t take kindly to this outsider in their lands. She fled for her life until she came into contact with a group of exploring mages who escorted her to the safety of Ulreich University. Days passed as she situated herself into her new life, vowing to herself that as soon as she was strong enough to return home she would.

And return she did.

Two years after her departure Kina took the journey back up the face of the mountain she appeared on with the company of several extremely talented and powerful mage friends. They crossed through the rift and targeted the inner circle that Alice held close to her who supplied power to the impenetrable barrier that protected the evil witch inside of London. Once they achieved their goal they rested for the night only to wake up to the cold corpse of one of their team members and trusted friends lying in a pool of their own blood still in bed. They had been murdered in their sleep which infuriated the group, fueling their passion until they encountered Alice whom had been caught between this band of mages coming from the south and the U.S. and Russian armies advancing upon them from the north. Without any means of escape the woman was forced to face her daughter in group combat only to be defeated and killed.

They returned back to Ulreich where Kina soon fell into a depressive spiral. For months she hid away in the safety and isolation of her dorm room, barely eating or interacting with the outside world. She went through a series of suicidal thoughts, but it hit her. Why kill herself? After all she'd been through, why put the people she loved in pain because of her own selfish desire to escape the guilt of killing her mother? She had done it for a good cause, it had taken its toll on her, but now she had a decision to make. End it all, or get back up and work through the pain, the suffering, to restore herself back to her former glory as a gifted Faith mage protecting and healing those in need. She chose the latter. Now she has achieved maximum rank, the apex of her power, and strives to promote peace and prosperity in the University.

Mana Manipulation
The most basic class of magic, involving the direct manipulation of Mana, is available to all mages. Raw Mana is a mundane source of magic with no special qualities, but offers some level of versatility. The amount of Mana a mage is capable of replenishing or expelling is dependent on their overall level.

Unique Archmage Mana Type
Earth Warden Mana is a combination of both her Faith Mana and her Geomancy Mana types. This mana is capable of being manifested in both physical and essence-based forms while also being capable of being infused into both sentient and non-sentient beings. Direct contact with Earth Warden mana in its base state without spells will automatically begin healing minor injuries such as scrapes, small cuts, bruises, etc., and when in its base form it may have a physical durability of up to iron strength. Can not be used to harm in any way, shape, or form.

Faith Mana
Mana which cannot do damage in any form. This mana is usually very warm and pleasant, and sometimes even gives off relaxing scents.

Earth Mana
Mana which manifests itself as dirt, mud, dust, etc.

Personalized Mana
Kina's mana, when visible to the naked eye, appears as an aura of bright gold, yet has a thin inner and outer border of a muted shade of brown that blends perfectly into the gold hues. Browns and other nude shades can be seen as small particles drifting inside of her aura.

Mana Weave
The simple act of moving one's mana pool through their own body. Channeling one's mana

Mana Spark
A basic mana projectile that can be used offensively or defensively. It causes no real damage other than a small sting when hit directly in the flesh.

Mana Pane
When focusing one's mana before them and swirling it in a vortex quickly and violently, one may allow their mana to harden, causing a thin pane to form. This spell can only be used defensively and the power it can block is minimal, ranking at iron toughness. This spell can be learned by anyone of mage rank and above, but upon reaching high mage, one may cast two of these at a time, one for each hand.

Mana Beacon
This spell is cast by concentrating on one's mind and projecting mana outwards like a sudden flash of light. Although this does not produce any visual effects, it sends a beacon-like pulse of mana outwards from ones body which can be picked up an interpreted by other mages who have learned this spell. It does not convey any message, rather a simple alarm. This can be used to send a cry for help to people within a set area of your character.

Spirit Beacon
An upgrade to Mana Beacon, rather than projecting one's mana from the mind in a pulse, this requires focus to be gathered to one's very core of being until released into the world around them. It scatters outward in a pulse like Mana Beacon, but is targeted specifically at Faith Mages, Necromancers and Spiritualists, intended to only be sensed by mages who follow those trees. While it opens up the possibility of being detected over a vaster distance (still within the same chat but a considerably larger range, with some very specific exceptions), it also offers the opportunity to warn aforementioned mages that a spirit may be disconnected from the body (recently or will be soon) and will require a guide back for revival. Upon reaching Expert Mage, focusing on this spell as it is being cast will allow the caster to choose which specific type of previously listed mage (Faith, Necromancer, Spiritualist) they desire to be able to sense their beacon.

Gel Sphere
By creating a mana spark and continuing to weave your mana around the spark like a web, eventually the orb one forms will expand into a small sphere of an incredibly adhesive substance. Once casted, this spell lasts for three posts, and has a toughness of aluminum.

Summon Medium
A spell that allows a user to transport their medium from a previous location into their hands. Only one medium can be used at a time with this spell, and can include HMM's, as well as basic objects and weapons. To change one's medium, one must use it at least once before being able to actively summon it.

Mana Meditation
This spell takes two posts, the first post charges the spell, the second post activates it. If a mage meditates on whatever is causing them pain or discomfort--meaning they focus their undivided attention inward or to whatever specific part of their body is hurting--and slowly weave their mana to that particular place, they will be able to activate this spell. Mana Meditation increases a mage's natural pain tolerance slightly and can calm whatever natural shock they may be experiencing, allowing them to function better. Natural shock meaning the universal spell won't work on magic based shock

Mana Rehabilitation
This spell takes two posts, the first post charges the spell, the second post activates it. If a mage temporarily focuses on whatever is causing them pain or discomfort--meaning they focus their most of their attention inward or to whatever specific part of their body is hurting or being affected by an external source--and weave their mana to that particular place, they will be able to activate this spell. Mana Rehabilitation increases a mage's natural pain tolerance by x1.5 and can calm whatever natural shock or magic based shock (spells that cause pain/shock/discomfort of up to Mage rank) that part of their body may be experiencing. (Can take a tiny bit of edge off higher ranked spells, but not very much, will mainly be ineffective to anything higher than mage rank.)

Mana Parry
In a complicated set of movements, the caster must create a solidified pane of mana upon an appendage or object, and swipe it against an incoming projectile. Upon contact, the caster has a split second to match their mana levels with the opposing spells, which causes the projectile to meld with the casters mana and remove all secondary effects from the energy. This causes the spell to fly off in a random direction, reverted to base mana but still powerful enough to do damage based on the rank of the attack. Casters who use this can only do so on spells of their rank and below, and to do so costs the exact same amount of mana as the spell in question, so one should keep this in mind before parrying anything. Projectiles that have special effects such as detonation, or abilities that can be triggered mid flight can be activated before a spell is able to be parried.

Mana Retaliate
An upgrade to Mana Parry, this technique requires the user to weave their magic into a point of contact (such as their hand or a medium) and double the amount of mana within an incoming projectile. Upon swiping the point of contact against the opposing force, the extra mana will override the attack and transform it into an orb-like blob of magic. The caster of Retaliate must push this slow-moving orb back toward them, and break it open before it makes contact with their body. This can be done by any basic projectile, such as mana parry, and will shatter the magic into three separate and more solid components. These shattered pieces are about the size of a fist, and shaped in an almost arrow-like manner; they fly at a relatively high speed in intricate and hard to follow patterns that eventually track the original attackers location for up to 60 feet away. Upon reaching five feet from their target, the three shards scatter, sending the brightest of the trio at the front of the attacker, while the other two dull projectiles attempt to pincer each of their sides. The frontal projectiles strength remains the same power as the original retaliated attack, and the two side projectiles work at about a rank lower. This cannot be used against projectiles above the casters rank.

Offensive Aura Alpha
Cast by concentrating your focus toward the very thing that gives you life. Although various creatures have vital organs in different areas, the most common place for the aura to originate from is the chest, directly in the center. For humans and other similar beings, the heart itself acts as the conduit. This aura is extremely volatile in nature, and appears very similar to flames covering the beings entirety, crackling and flaring haphazardly around them.Takes two turns to cast (one turn to prepare weaving mana into the epicenter, and one post allowing the aura to fully take effect). With the aura up, it is able to provide a slight boost (x1.5) to a person's physical strength, as well as their casting speed (this does not work for spells that require multiple posts to charge), and spell damage (around x1.25 more power, does not increase the rank of spells). The aura will make a person feel extremely warm and accelerated, creatures who possess hearts and similar organs will find that they will beat extremely fast while the aura is up. This aura will last for a period of four psts, and can be upheld for another four by charging it with mana (expending the cost to bring it up over again).

Utility Aura Alpha
Cast by concentrating on the mind itself, both in a figurative and literal sense. Casters will start by weaving mana directly into their brains, allowing the aura to expand throughout their entire body, flowing directly from the top of their heads. The spell takes one post to charge, and is cast on the users next turn. The aura will take a very liquid-like appearance while this spell is cast, flowing downwards over the body in a smooth, gentle motion. The aura will glow lightly, being slightly visible in direct light, but bright enough to glow during the darkness. With the aura up, it increases a being's reflexes and overall agility. The aura will make a person feel extremely awake and alert, and increase their visual perception slightly. This aura will last for a period of four posts, and can be upheld for another four by charging it with mana (expending the cost to bring it up over again).

Cast by concentrating on the very thing that keeps you standing. In most cases, this means the caster must begin by weaving mana directly into their spine, and allow it to flow into the rest of their body. This aura takes an extremely solid and rigid appearance; it will form around your entire body like a suit of armor, and does not contain an ethereal glow, rather a shine very similar to polished metal. Firing the shards (even on a straight path) is not possible unless the user is within 45 of the caster themselves.
Individual shards used in the “buff” on allies can counter a single spell of Post High Mage Rank 1 (in the same way a barrier would counter a spell) if the shard makes contact with the spell. This does not apply for Post High Mage Rank 1 spells that have been charged for multiple rotations before being cast. All five shards must be used against the same Post High Mage Rank 2 spell for them to succeed in countering it. With area of effect spells, they will not counter the entire thing, simply passing through it and tanking whatever they come into contact with assuming that the spell isn’t too strong and to cancel it completely they have to make physical contact with the source. This is a similar case for magically enhanced items (Enchanted items, Ritualism spells, Wizardry Artifacts, specialized Mediums, etc etc) spells where the mage imbues an object with their mana. In order to counter a spell like that, area of effect or not, the shards must physically come into contact with the source: I.E. the rune being used ( unless engraved into the body ) and the item which is enchanted to actually affect it. This will not affect energy based spells (does not include spells which have no solid form such as Aeromancy spells) or Unity Summons/Puppets. This will, however, be especially good against Necromancy summons of up to Post High Mage rank 1. If a shard can embed itself into a Necromancy summon of Post High Mage rank 1 or less without being destroyed or destroying the summon altogether, then Kina herself can expend more mana with that shard as a conduit and use powerful “cleansing/healing” power from her Faith magic to destroy it. This will also destroy the shard, and she is incapable of doing so if the shard is more than 50 feet away from her.
Earth Warden Spells

Faith


Faith mages are a completely support-oriented group of mages. Those who practice Faith hold the ability to mend wounds, lift curses and illnesses, bestow temporary magical boons to their allies, or create Holy Equipment which provide a permanent boon. Faith mages are also highly skilled in defensive arts, and are capable of erecting magical barriers. However, faith mana is completely non hostile, and no faith spell can ever cause harm to another being (unless faith magic specifically harms them).

Heal
By projecting her mana through the palms of her hands, she floods the recipients body with white mana. The mana mends broken bones, internal bleeding, flesh wounds, and the like. The larger and more devastating the wound, the more mana the spell takes. This can not regenerate lost limbs.

Cleanse
Another spell transmitted via touch. Cleanse targets both magic and physical ailments such as induced blindness, poisons, curses, and other effects. This spell can only cure spells equal to or less than Kina's rank. Does not affect spells she has no knowledge of.

Fortitude
Blesses an ally target, increasing their physical abilities by 1.5x -- capping at 3x ( strength, endurance, vitality, speed ) and also doubles their pain tolerance. Lasts for five posts and requires some form of contact to cast on someone as well as requiring the target to be within 25 feet of her to retain their buff.

Angelic Bulwark
By extending mana from their core the caster may create a dimly lit golden bubble around themselves capable of reaching 10 feet wide and tall. The barrier itself is made out of Titanium magical construct walls specially designed to allow spells to be cast from within the bubble but prevent them from coming inside. The Bulwark is capable of defending against two equal strength attacks at any point of its smooth dome before breaking and is completely solid when trying to come INTO the bubble.

Sanctuary
Sending a burst of Faith mana into the ground, Kina blesses it in a thirty foot radius around her body, causing the ground to crack in a spiderweb pattern with light bursting from the cracks. While coating the bodies of selected targets whom are bathed in said light, it surrounds their body in a skin-tight suit of highly flexible and durable faith mana which acts as a defensive barrier. A maximum of five targets may be affected by this barrier at one time so long as they stay within the radius of the spell, and it gets progressively stronger depending on how many people it is being cast on while varying in overall mana cost for the same reason. I.E. 2 people get titanium-grade barriers, three people will get cobalt barriers, and five people will all get steel barriers. Each barrier may absorb up to two attacks of its respective durability before breaking, and while affected by a barrier the target will receive a visionary buff that allows them to see through lesser ranking darkness also based on the strength of their shield ( lower grade barriers can see through darkness of their own durability rank or lower for up to an extra ten feet in front of them in complete darkness regardless of their barrier strength. ) Like the visionary buff, the spell itself emits a light which can pierce through darkness of equal rank or lower, unable to do so through stronger darkness, and will illuminate the area a good few feet around each crack just enough to be allow for basic -- albeit limited -- vision.

Infusion
At the expense of the caster’s own mana by charging for one post and releasing it in a burst in a twenty foot radius around her, those hit will acquire a buff that allows them to use mana more efficiently, to use more spells without wasting as much mana. Infusion drops the average casting cost of the user’s next spells cast by one rank if below High Mage, or even two ranks if the user is equal to or below Mage rank. Infusion may affect up to four people at one time, who all have to stay within a thirty foot radius of the caster to keep their buff. This buff lasts for four posts, and rather than just buffing their next spell, it reduces the cost of all future spells cast while this buff is active on them, and requires one post to cool down.

Apotheosis
A powerful buff that may be applied to defensive barriers, rather than a person, this spell causes a strong barrier over a pre-existing one through required contact with it via one of her own spells or direct contact with her body. This barrier allows the previous one to absorb up to two extra attacks of High Mage ranking before breaking. The caster may spread this buff across two separate barriers, but this will only be able to give each individual defensive construct one stack of the buff. I.E. 1 shield - 2 Titanium attacks. 2 shields - 1 Titanium attack each. If the caster leaves a fifteen foot radius of the buffed barrier then the buff will deactivate prematurely. Apotheosis lasts for four posts and then goes on a two post cool down timer.

Sensory Pulse
By releasing a pulse of faith mana into the target, the spell works like echolocation. The mana bounces off of every part of them and returns to Kina, allowing her to "see" any internal and external injuries such as internal bleeding, respiratory problems, etc. This spells works to find both physical and magic induced problems and is able to detect harmful spells of up to HM+ rank. Does not detect spells that are intentionally hidden by the caster.

Faith Spells

Geomancy


A subcategory of the Elementalism tree, Geomancers specialize in the manipulation and manifestation of Earthen-based spells such as stones. They may start off weak with only stones and rubble at their disposal, but as they grow in strength they grow in power to be able to manipulate metals, gemstones, or even magma.

Wall
Selecting a point within fifteen feet of the caster’s current position, this spell erects a barrier composed of any earthen material within the caster’s skill level including sand, mud, or stone. This wall is six feet tall, four feet wide and three feet thick. The wall formed may only be up to Iron in strength, and when using pre-existing materials that are not this strong will then be coated in a layer of mana that increases it up to Iron strength rather than being below. No matter which type of wall is formed, they will all be able to withstand one attack of mage rank before breaking. If not broken, it will last for three posts before automatically breaking down.

Earthen Needles
Manifests the caster’s Geomancy mana into a physical form, taking on the shape of a jagged needle of iron-grade rock. These needles are equal to iron in strength and are capable of piercing through iron constructs. Earthen Needles are projectiles which travel at up to twenty miles per hour as well as being able to travel for up to thirty feet and up to four may be summoned at a time. They last for two posts before automatically breaking down.

Spikes
Akin to her Wall spell, Kina must first select a singular point within a fifteen foot radius of her current position before manifesting her geomancy mana into a physical form. This time, it takes the shape of a sharp, jagged conic spike constructed of iron-grade rock. This spike is three feet in diameter at the base, but at its maximum height of five feet it ends in a sharp point capable of piercing straight through iron-grade constructs. These spikes last for two posts before automatically breaking down but may be retracted on command and extended once again but only in the original path that it had.

Sandstorm
By expelling torrents of sand from the palms of her hand, she manipulates the sand created and whirls it around her body like a twister along with any debris it can pick up. This spell is capable of dealing light skin damage once per post, and heavily reducing eyesight for up to thirty feet from her. Sandstorm lasts for three posts but requires to be channeled for one post while emitting sand from the palms.

Rock Armament
Focusing her mana around her fists, she wills it to become her tree's respective physical element: Rock. This causes rock to form around her fists, molding to the shape of her hands to form large gauntlets of solid stone which have the durability of Iron. She is able to flex her hands within the gauntlet, which in turn causes the gauntlet to follow her movements like any other normal set of gloves. If she is able to connect a solid punch, the weight behind the punch could easily break bones. The gauntlets are easily fourty each, but to her they feel closer to ten pounds. Can break through Iron defenses with multiple hits.

Seeking Stone
Requiring a great deal of focus and energy, the caster begins to manipulate the ground beneath them (so long as the surface they are touching does not exceed iron in strength) to cave in and open up like a tunnel. This begins slowly and exponentially grows in speed over one post, allowing the caster to reach depths of up to ten feet under the ground and expand the tunnel to fit up to two people side by side at once. While not having a set distance the tunnel may go, it takes a hefty chunk of mana once per post to steadily maintain all of the moved soil. However, by expanding extra mana the caster may make the soil lock together into a solid around the roof of the tunnel and increase it’s durability to iron. This allows the tunnel to take one attack of iron durability before it begins to collapse. Seeking Stone lasts for a maximum of two posts before it begins to automatically collapse. Anything standing inside of it when it collapses will quickly be crushed underneath the weight of it.
